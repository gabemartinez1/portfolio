Emi Embroidery

Brand design for small embroidery business.

Overview

Emi Embroidery is a new small embroidery business that focused on custom order pieces stitched on pre-loved garments and repurposed textiles. Mainly operating through Instagram (check it out here!), Emi Embroidery did not have a cohesive or distinct brand identity. This project was the first step in establishing a clear brand as the business gains traction.

Inspiration

I worked with the client to brainstorm brand adjectives and create a mood board to visualize how we wanted the brand to look and feel.

Brand Adjectives

Artistic
Empowering
Playful
Sustainable

Mood Board

Brand Colors

I pulled brand colors from the mood board - the more natural green, blueish gray, and pale yellow work together to portray sustainability. The salmon pink contrasts to pull in playfulness and artistry.

Typography

I selected two fonts that paired well and presented them with examples of text in action to show how typography should be used across print and web media.

Logo and Submarks

I avoided creating an overly stylized or illustrated logo because I did not want to distract from the artistic designs on the actual products. Instead, I opted for a simple typographic logo using the selected brand fonts. I used lowercase lettering to give a casual and creative vibe and played with the letter e in both Emi and Embroidery for one of the submarks.

Packaging

I mocked up some potential packaging designs for the brand, opting for reusable tote bags and paper tags. Brand packaging or merchandise should align with the value of sustainability.
